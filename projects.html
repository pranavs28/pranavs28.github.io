The COVID Report

The COVID Report was a project started in March of 2020 to create a platform for communication about the COVID-19 outbreak. Starting as a project to increase awareness, the podcast grew to a daily schedule in order to provide a summary of daily news regarding the pandemic and its impacts on public health, politics, and economics.

The podcast is available on all streaming platforms including Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, and many more. Take a look with the included Anchor player or subscribe on your favorite podcasting platform! Follow The COVID Report on social media @thecovidreport.
